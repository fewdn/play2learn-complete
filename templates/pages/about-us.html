About the Play2Learn Final Project

Students are given a partial Django template wired with a couple VueJS games. Students are tasked with using Django to register new users, log users in and out, allow users to track their game scores, and submit reviews. All Site visitors can play games without an account but their scores are not tracked. All site visitors can submit a contact form.

The final project combines the Django & Postgres backend training with the frontend HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, & VueJS training completed earlier in the course.
